Info 340AB Group 4 Project Proposal

The field of performing arts is rich in diversity and cultural significance. And in this spotlight of mainstream large-scale theatrical productions, many hidden gems are overlooked: these smaller performances, which include theater, dance, music, and other forms of performing arts, are often organized by local groups, student organizations, and independent artists. Despite their great potential and cultural value, these productions struggle to gain visibility and audience impact. This is largely due to the dominance of established systematic performances in the present day. The visibility gap reflects a number of problems: many potential audiences are unaware of these great local arts. And those vibrant artists are missing out on the opportunity to showcase their talents to the world. Mainstream promotional platforms tend to prioritize higher profile, more commercially viable shows. These smaller shows are underrepresented. This not only affects their viability, but also limits the cultural diversity of communities. We're bridging this gap with the Fringe Theater Calendar website. The platform would specifically address the needs of fringe theaters, student groups, and other small performing arts organizations by providing them with a centralized space to list their events. The site will provide comprehensive information about each performance, including time, location, promotional posters, show themes, performer descriptions, and links to purchase tickets. In addition, it will include a basic introduction to each performance type to help those unfamiliar with a particular art form. This approach not only addresses the issue of visibility for smaller productions, but also serves as an educational tool for the community, potentially expanding the audience base for these art forms. The site aims to address underrepresentation and the lack of diverse cultural experiences. It seeks to fill specific gaps that have not been addressed by existing platforms, thereby fostering a more inclusive and vibrant local arts scene.
